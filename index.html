The Ladies - Sophie and Sybil

Well Small Animal Rescue
(Near Spilsby, Lincolnshire).

Ali at WSAR first started rescuing animals back in the early 80's. They have limited space but are able to continue with small animal rescue (cats, kittens, rodents, rabbits) on a small scale.

"We are a non-profit making sanctuary relying solely on donations and fund raising events such as online auctions and garage/car boot sales to raise funds for veterinary bills, upkeep and neutering to keep going."

"We are a NO KILL - NO BREEDING and we also trap, neuter and release (TNR) cats from local feral colonies when required. When a cat or kitten comes in to our care we owe it to do the best that we can to ensure from that day on it will be cared for properly. Most of the cats and kittens we take in are unwanted and have had miserable lives, to ensure they all leave us for excellent homes for we have a re-homing criteria for anyone wishing to adopt."

Fund Raising

Did you know that whenever you buy anything online – from your weekly shop to your annual holiday – you could be raising a free donation for Well Small Animal Rescue?
There are nearly 3,000 retailers including Amazon, John Lewis, Aviva, thetrainline and Sainsbury’s, who will donate a percentage of the amount you spend to WSAR to say thank you for shopping with them.
It’s really simple, and doesn’t cost you anything.
All you have to do is:
1. Go to WSAR page at easyfundraising
2. Sign up for free
3. Get shopping – your donations will be collected by easyfundraising and automatically sent to WSAR.

It couldn’t be easier!
There are no catches or hidden charges and Ali will be really grateful for your donations.
Thank you for your support.
